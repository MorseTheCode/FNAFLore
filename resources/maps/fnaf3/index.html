FNaF 3 Maps

Night 1 Night 2 Night 3 Night 4 Night 5

Getting maps created for these games are a tedious process. I need to progress in the game itself, screenshotting each minigame/map, take that screenshot into Paint.NET and overlay source images over the screenshots. This produces clear, crisp maps free of scanlines.

Therefore, it is no surprise that these take a long time to sort out. and that’s without applying animation to the images, which is an altogether different challenge!

As such, this section is not yet finished. It will be in time, but I will need to actually progress through the games, and do the post processing on the images! Thank you for bearing with me as I try to complete this process!
